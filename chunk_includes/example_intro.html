This experiment is a rather simple one:
  1. You are shown a couple of sentences word by word. Read them at your own natural pace, and try to grasp the exact meaning of each sentence.
  2. You can advance to the next word by pressing the space bar.
  3. Every now and then, a question pops up. Please choose the correct answer! Should you choose the wrong answer, we will let you know. Should you choose the right answer though, you will not get any message.
  4. Please do NOT refresh or go back. In that case, your progress would be lost and you would have to start over.
  5. While reading, you will see a link to the instructions on top of the page, in case you need to refer to them at some point.
